Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a job?

Go to "Post New Job" from the sidebar, fill in all the required details, and click "Publish Job". Your job will be live immediately.

How do I manage applicants?

Go to "Applicants" section to view all candidates who applied. You can filter by job and status, and shortlist or reject candidates.

Can I edit a job after posting?

Yes, go to "My Jobs" and click on the job you want to edit. You can update any details or close the job if needed.

How do candidates apply?

Candidates can see your posted jobs, view details, and click "Apply Now". Their applications will appear in your Applicants section.

Can I close a job posting?

Yes, from "My Jobs" click "Close Job". You can always reopen it later if needed.
